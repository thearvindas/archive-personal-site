my sister's arangetram, wrapping up 6th sem, and running a 10K

May 15 2022

Sixth sem has been absolute madness. Hear me out.

March 10th was the last time I wrote something for this blog. It’s crazy how fast time has gone by. I have my final exams going on right now (I should definitely be studying Heat Transfer at this very moment) but I decided I’ll put out some time to update my life, as I’ve done so far, over here. (I genuinely wonder if people read this, and if they do, what goes through their heads when they read my thoughts. If you’re reading this, I would love to talk with you and understand. Please hit me up on Twitter =P)

This sem has been incredibly hectic and challenging. Shifting back to an offline mode of college has been pretty taxing, add to that regular assignments, exams, and labs — so very tiring. While I’m incredibly grateful that we could come back to campus, I can’t wait for this sem to end, I do need a break.

From automobiles to thermodynamics to marketing to first aid, I’ve learnt so much this semester. Along with college, I’ve been working at Zuperly as well. Incredible to think of the progress we’ve made as a team and the product we’ve built from scratch over the last few months. I wrap up my internship this week, but so much gratitude for the learnings and the opportunities I’ve got here. This has truly kickstarted the journey for me. Thank you so much Kapil and team.

I’m so excited to let you know that a month ago, my sister had her Bharatnatyam arangetram. So proud of what she’s achieved over the last 10 years. She’s had so many ups and downs but she’s never given up, what a performer, what an inspiration, and what a reminder to persist.

I’m also so excited to tell you that I finished my third 10K run! Oh man, this was really such a push, I’ve been working out quite a bit, but this run had me on the edge. Especially towards the end, the track had immense elevation changes, absolute death towards the final few kilometres. So so happy I kept pushing, there’s NO other feeling like crossing the finish line after working so hard. I’m confident I can reach 21K very soon. Just need to put in the effort. I think, more than effort, need to put in the time. Running long distances takes a lot of time, just can’t seem to find enough of it with so much on my plate. I know what I’ll be doing this summer.

I have just a year of engineering left. We’re done. So close to the end. It’s almost crazy to think about. I’m really happy though. This phase of life has been a tremendous learning experience. Now we really need to figure things out. I might have to move out of the hostels next so I need to figure out how to rent an apartment. I need to get an internship in my 8th semester. And I need to figure out where I’m taking my career after college. We’ll get it done though.

One year ago, at this time, I was nowhere. I had no vision of what I wanted to be, I had no goals. I can say I’ve come a long way. I don’t know what the future holds in store, but I can’t wait to learn and grow more. So much more to come, I really can’t wait. Here’s to more effort, more pushing, and reaching those heights.

The sky is not the limit.

Peace out.
