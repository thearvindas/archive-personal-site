upraised, embark, and a small step ahead

Nov 3 2021


I’ve always been an ardent fan of trusting the process. It’s easy to get disheartened when things don’t seem to work out but it’s important to believe that everyone has a story, a process, a journey towards what they want to achieve — trust it and never stop the hard work.

Yesterday came the good news of me clearing Upraised’s internship program called Embark . I was selected as one of the top 50 in the Design track among a total of 4.5L participants. I couldn’t be more excited and grateful. Huge huge shoutout to my good friend Avaneesh who told me about Embark, wouldn’t have done this without him :D

It’s not the biggest deal, but it’s a start. Small steps in the direction I want. Clearing the exam is only a small part of the joy, can’t tell you how excited I am to start training and ultimately, interning next summer. The biggest win is the opening up of new opportunities to learn and progress further up the ladder.

That’s it really. Just wanted to update this over here. Really happy, and like I’ve said before, miles and miles to go before I sleep.

Huge thanks to my family for the constant support, to my friends who treat my wins as theirs, and to Upraised for the great opportunity.

Can’t wait to see what comes next.
Peace out.
