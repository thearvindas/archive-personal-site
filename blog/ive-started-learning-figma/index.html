I've Started Learning Figma

Jun 21 2021

The more I’ve been exploring the world of design, the more I’ve realized the immense power of Figma. Naturally, I had to get my hands dirty right away.

I play around with Adobe Ps and Ai quite often, so it didn’t take too much time to get going with Figma. I was immediately amazed by the brilliant workflow, tools, and simply the experience of using it. I watched a few YouTube videos to understand how things work. Right after that, I decided to start building. @abnux says copying and recreating a pre-existing design is a great way to start. Who am I to question the advice of this legend? I decided to do just that.

Which pre-existing design, though? I looked through my phone — Spotify is one of my most-used apps. It was a no-brainer from there.

I recreated the splash-screen and home page. It’s not much, but I really enjoyed it. Surprisingly, it looks very similar to the actual design, which is a damn good feeling. I’m very proud of how this turned out. I hope to take it away from here.
