a recap, my first internship, and the pursuit of happyness

Dec 24 2021

Since a lot of what I’ve wrote down in this blog is my attempt at getting an internship, it makes sense to have a post celebrating that I’ve finally got one :D

Stoked to announce that I’ve joined Zuperly as a Product intern! It’s been nearly 2 weeks since I’ve joined and I’ve had a fantastic experience so far. Huge huge thanks to the founder Kapil who’s made it so so easy for me to jump in on this and get started.

It’s just the beginning so I won’t talk about this too much, but can’t wait to share my learnings somewhere down the line.

When I look back over the last few months, I think there’s been a lot of takeaways with respect to getting internships, putting your best foot out there, and jumping in on opportunities. When I look back at the first product interview I gave, I see a lot of things I’d have done differently, both in terms of prep as well as mindset. Perhaps, the good thing about recaps and retrospection is being able to pick up some positives and learnings. Have some regrets here and there, but nothing stronger than the gratitude for everything this year has given me. Can’t wait for 2022!

I spent last night watching The Pursuit Of Happyness with my sister. What a fantastic movie, absolute tear-jerking from the start to end. These are the kinds of movies I like — raw story-telling coupled with brilliant characters. That’s really all I need in movies. If you have suggestions for similar stories that I should watch, do hit me up :P

And if you’re still reading…
Try out Zuperly! We’re building a platform that will help you take your learnings to the next level. People-first tech, where you are most important. Can guarantee it’ll help you out massively, so do give it a shot :D

Until next time.
Peace out.
