About Me

“The more I live, the more I learn. The more I learn, the more I realize, the less I know.” – Michel Legrand

I’m Arvin Das, a 20 y/o currently living in Hyderabad, India. By day, I’m a third year Mechatronics undergrad at MIT, Manipal. By night, I’m a sucker for design, minimalism, productivity and growth. On the weekends, you’ll find me rooting for Red Bull Racing.

Since the start of 2021, I’ve developed a deep interest in designing great visuals. I like combining functionality and sleek design to build products users will love.

I’m driven by the desire to learn something new every day.
